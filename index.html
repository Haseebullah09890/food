Tradition on a Grill

Donisl isn't just a restaurant — it's a culinary journey through the rich heritage of Pakistani BBQ. Founded in the heart of North Nazimabad, we've spent over a decade perfecting the art of flame-grilled delicacies, aromatic karahis, and contemporary fast food favorites.

Our master chefs bring generations of expertise, using only premium halal meats, hand-picked spices, and time-honored recipes passed down through tradition.
